Typography

The style utilizes a dynamic, hand-drawn brush script.
It features a rhythmic, bouncy baseline where the letters dance slightly, visually mimics the energy,
music, and physical movement inherent in a live festival environment.
It is a flexible and classic approach to represent a timeless festivity like Tiringbanay.

Color Study

This palette transforms a festival into a dreamscape. The Midnight Navy (#1a0159) acts as a deep sonic void, allowing the Electric Cyan (#00baff) and Hot Magenta (#f4008a) to cut through like laser beams and glowing light sticks.
The Vivid Blue (#006df4) and Royal Purple (#740091) create a seamless, immersive gradient of movement, while the Bright Yellow (#fff554) serves as the "spark"—mimicking the flash of face paint and the burst of bubbles under a spotlight. It is high-contrast, high-energy, and purely celebratory.
